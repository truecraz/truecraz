Nuvo Network

Claudia Zingone, Daniela DeJong, Amanda Fiorini, Tanvi Chhabra, Lin Xuefei

About

This research will focus on the relation between co-working and the online presence of NUVO Network. We believe that an accessible space where members can interact with other members would elevate the community aspect of the company. During the tour of NUVO we were told the the company wished to have a calendar-like screen in the atrium displaying general information about who has booked what, events occurring, and etc. To resolve this issue, we propose developing a website that all members of Nuvo has access to. No matter where an individual may be or what they are looking for, this space is where members can collaborate, ask questions, have conversations, book workspaces, and more. Interactivity and community involvement are key.

In conducting this research, we intend to use various methods of research to collect information from different members of NUVO Network (primary source), as well as other company examples of interactive forums (secondary sources). We will use this information to develop a website that best suits the needs of NUVO Network.

Reseach Question

How can we improve how NUVO interacts with tenants and potential mem bers
through their social media platforms?

Goal

Create a welcoming first impression of NUVO
To invite more people to share and connect
Promoting a active and thriving community to those passing by

Methods

Fly on the Wall

This method requires us to observe and record behaviour in their context, with interfering with people’s activities; this method helps us see what people do within real contexts and time frames, helping us understand how the members at Nuvo interact with each other in the environment. Observing users in the environment we’re designing a service for will help us identify what tasks people want to accomplish in Nuvo, without bringing in our own opinions on what they should be doing.

Brainstorming/Mind Mapping

Mind mapping will help us organize our thoughts and make connections between information. It will allow us to judge and make assumptions against all our possible ideas and strategies and combine ideas. The visual outline of our thought process can lead to new concepts we have not considered and the expansion of our current strategies. It can also help identify our strongest and weakest links.

Secondary Research

Using information that other people have already put together with their own research. Also using publicly available information to better inform our studies.

Competitive Product Analysis

Product surveys help us understand successful tactics employed by other competitors in the same or similar fields.

Interviews with Nuvo Staff and Intercept Interviews

The interview will be a very important aspect of our research still we will be able to get an account of who things work within the company and have the opinions of the staff and members. We will be able to create profiles based of of real people who attend these facilities. The interviews let us observe the reactions and the emotions of individual people and how they feel about the company. With this information we are capable of obtaining new and updated information on the company and members.

Paper Prototyping

A method widely used when designing human-computer interactions, paper prototyping helps us create software that meets the user's expectations and needs, especially in the case of user interfaces. It will be used when designing concepts for our website and finding the best functionality for its potential users. We would be experimenting with different concepts of design and testing how understandable the website would be for the average person to use.

Surveys

Data collection through questionnaires, this method will focus on replies from students in creative fields who are most likely to use cooperative working spaces in the future.

Conclusion

In conclusion, by analyzing other social media platforms of other co working spaces, we have discovered that using all features of the platform (stories, hashtags, reposting, etc.) can really increase community involvement and overall traffic of the platform. Should NUVO choose to implement these ideas, we believe they could benefit significantly.
